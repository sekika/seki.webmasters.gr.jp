Shogi and Chess variant

On The Chess Variant Pages maintained by Hans Bodlaender, several shogi variants are introduced. On this website, I have written rules for two famous shogi variants: Hasami Shogi and Southern Shogi.

I also maintain Japanese translations of the chess variant pages, where I have translated following rules.

  • 007 Chess
  • Alice Chess
  • Atomic Chess
  • Avalanche Chess
  • Bennekom Double Move Chess
  • Berolina Chess
  • Highcastle chess
  • Makruk: Thai Chess
  • N-Relay Chess
  • Self Eliminator
  • Single Check Chess
  • Taxi Chess
  • Three Kinds of Billiards Chess
  • Tilting the Balance Chess
  • Upside-down chess
  • WWII Chess
